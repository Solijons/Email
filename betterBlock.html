ABOUT US
 A difficult medical diagnosis is likely to be one of the most significant events in your life. The impact to your personal well-being, as well as that of those who love and care about you, is profound. We believe that patient communities can play a crucial role in navigating this difficult time. Better Block specifically seeks to fill a void in localized patient networks. Despite today’s overwhelming landscape of messaging tools and social platforms, there is no clear vehicle through which patients can connect and share localized support and guidance throughout their medical journey. Better Block is unique in that it creates a patient community that is both tech-enabled and hyper-local. We bridge the gap between modern ‘at-your-fingertips’ social platforms and the antiquated patient support programs available in today’s healthcare system.

OUR VALUES
 We believe in COMMUNITY - The digital age has redefined community. Let us also redefine - and improve - our offline community. We will all become stronger and healthier when we do. We believe in the POWER OF TECHNOLOGY - No more waiting for a weekly hospital support group. No more scanning online forums. A woman just a few blocks away was in your shoes last year. She is just a few clicks away. We believe in PRIVACY - You medical story, and the conversations you have about it, are private. It should remain between you and the people you actively choose to share it with. We promise to respect that.
